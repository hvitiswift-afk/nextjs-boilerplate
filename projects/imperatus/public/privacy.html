Privacy Policy

The review build uses simulated transport only and does not contact an SMS provider or carrier.

Raw phone numbers, device identifiers, provider credentials, MFA codes, and unrelated conversation data must not be placed in prompts, public logs, or test fixtures.

Receipts should retain only the minimum state, hashes, timestamps, and non-sensitive evidence needed for review.

No user data is sold. A future production provider would require a separate provider-specific disclosure before live capability could be considered.
