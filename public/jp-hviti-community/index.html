Community rules

  • Participation is voluntary, opt-in, and reversible.
  • No scraping, purchased lists, hidden enrollment, or unsolicited mass outreach.
  • No harassment, threats, hate speech, impersonation, or pressure to contribute.
  • Financial contributions do not represent equity, securities, ownership, or guaranteed return.
  • Norstein access is limited by actual readiness, safety, confidentiality, and permission.
  • Personal information and recognition preferences must be respected.
  • Claims about progress, capabilities, payments, and access should remain accurate and verifiable.
  • Moderation decisions prioritize safety, consent, usefulness, and continuity.
