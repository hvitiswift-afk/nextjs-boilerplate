# GRIPLOOM Contract Pack

This document is a practical contract checklist and drafting starter. It is not legal advice. Use legal review before relying on it commercially.

## 1. Client Service Agreement

Purpose:

GRIPLOOM provides production-geometry reports built from public, licensed, or permissioned credit/source data.

Deliverables may include:

- PDF report.
- Spreadsheet or CSV.
- Production geometry graph.
- Source notes.
- QR/barcode exports.
- Optional Fire TV / display view.

## 2. Scope Clause

The client must provide one of the following:

- One crew member.
- Three productions.
- A separately quoted larger scope.

Out-of-scope work requires a new quote.

## 3. Data Use Terms

GRIPLOOM may use:

- Public credits.
- Licensed datasets.
- Permissioned client materials.
- Client-provided source notes.

GRIPLOOM should not use:

- Restricted databases without permission.
- Private personal data unrelated to the report.
- Unauthorized copyrighted footage or confidential production documents.

## 4. Source and Rights Disclaimer

GRIPLOOM reports summarize and analyze source-linked credit information. They do not replace official credits, guild records, union records, legal chain-of-title documents, or production-company records.

## 5. No Hiring Guarantee Clause

GRIPLOOM identifies collaboration signals and production-geometry patterns. It does not guarantee worker performance, hiring outcomes, future availability, crew reliability, legal eligibility, or production success.

## 6. No Legal / Union Determination Clause

GRIPLOOM does not provide legal advice, union determinations, employment determinations, rights opinions, or chain-of-title conclusions.

## 7. Confidentiality Clause

If the client provides private materials, GRIPLOOM will treat them as confidential and use them only for the agreed project unless otherwise authorized.

## 8. Payment / Refund Clause

Starter Pack pricing may be fixed. Larger scopes require a quote.

If GRIPLOOM cannot verify enough information to complete the agreed scope, GRIPLOOM may offer:

- A narrowed scope.
- A substitute target.
- A partial or full refund depending on work completed.

## 9. Security and Breach Notice Clause

GRIPLOOM will use reasonable safeguards for client materials. If client-provided confidential information is confirmed exposed, GRIPLOOM will notify affected clients when required or appropriate.

## 10. Publication Permission Clause

Client reports are private by default unless the client grants permission to use excerpts, anonymized findings, or sample graphics for marketing.

## Fixed Footer

GRIPLOOM maps production geometry from verified credits. Findings are source-linked, non-causal collaboration signals only.
