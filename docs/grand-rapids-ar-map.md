# Grand Rapids AR Map

Norstein Bekkler Grand Rapids AR Map is a free-core city guide.

It begins in Grand Rapids, Michigan and can be used as a portable take-home experience.

The experience is free. No purchase is required. No tip is required.

Use public places and public routes only. Do not trespass, block businesses, enter private property, track private locations, or share private location/payment information.

## Start

Start with the Downtown Free-Core Gate, then choose one route:

- Downtown Core
- River Route
- Museum Lore Route
- Eastown / Wealthy Route
- Heritage Hill Route
- Fulton / Market Route
- Medical Mile Route
- John Ball Route

## Silver Points

Silver Points are zero-value game tokens for lore and route progress. They do not expire and have no cash value.

## Carry It With You

You can leave with the map as a free-core guide. No payment or tip is required.
