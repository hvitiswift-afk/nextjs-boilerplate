# Midwest AR Expansion

Grand Rapids, Michigan is the origin model for the Norstein Bekkler free-core AR map.

## Expansion Route

- Grand Rapids: origin gate; river, museum, arcade, free-core map.
- Lansing: capital signal; civic relay and state-route connector.
- Detroit: industrial music gate; motors, sound, river, night signal.
- Chicago: lake megacity gate; train lines, skyline, theater, regional portal.
- Midwest: portable route network.
- National Model: city-by-city free-core public-route AR model.

## Rules

The model remains free-core everywhere:

- No purchase required.
- No tip required.
- No payment required for access.
- No card collection.
- No private-location tracking.
- Public routes only.
- No trespass.
- Silver Points have no cash value and do not expire.

## City Template

Every city page should include:

1. City name.
2. Public-route zones.
3. Safety/privacy rules.
4. Free-core statement.
5. Take-home card text.
6. Optional original/licensed soundscape notes.
7. Rollback path.
8. ChatGPT-assisted note without claiming official OpenAI endorsement.
