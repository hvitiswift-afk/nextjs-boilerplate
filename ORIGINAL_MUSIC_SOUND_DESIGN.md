# Original Music and Sound Design Guide

This guide prepares the Norstein Bekkler sound world without using unlicensed music.

## Target Mood

- faint entry music
- cosmic signal
- space battle pressure
- eerie horror
- macabre carnival
- gritty thriller
- adventure motion
- fun route energy
- hidden pipe room tone
- musty arcade nostalgia

## Original Composition Direction

Create original music inspired by the requested moods, not copied recordings or melodies.

### Entry Loop

A slow synth pulse, distant bells, low pipe drone, soft arcade shimmer, and light footstep rhythm.

### Cosmic Terror Layer

Low brass-like synths, reversed cymbal swells, distant starship rumble, and warning-beacon pulses.

### Horror / Macabre Layer

Prepared-piano hits, detuned music-box notes, cold reverb, and faint carnival organ textures.

### Adventure / Fun Layer

Warm bass movement, simple drum pulse, bright chime motifs, and route-completion stingers.

## Use Rule

Use original, licensed, or user-directed playback only. Do not claim artist approval until confirmed.
