AI operations that stay under human control.

Fardarter Systems turns complex work into structured, reviewable action packets with source tracking, approval gates, receipts, rollback controls, and continuity across tools.

View the pilot founder@fardarter.systems

The problem

AI-assisted work often becomes scattered across chats, email, documents, calendars, financial records, and technical tools. Facts mix with assumptions, receipts disappear, and planning can be mistaken for execution.

The product

Fardarter is a human-approved operations layer that converts fuzzy instructions into clear action packets, preserves evidence and uncertainty, and blocks external execution until the exact action is reviewed and approved.

The controls

Every external action is tied to a verified target, authority check, version lock, receipt plan, rollback path, and final human approval.
