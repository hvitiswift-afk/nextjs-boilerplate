## Founder description

I am an independent founder and systems architect building Fardarter Systems, a human-controlled AI operations platform. It converts complex personal and business workflows into reviewable action packets with source tracking, explicit approval gates, version locking, receipts, rollback controls, and continuity across tools. I am developing the prototype around OpenAI-powered task interpretation, structured outputs, retrieval, tool routing, evaluations, and human approval checkpoints.

## Problem

AI-assisted work is often scattered across chats, email, documents, calendars, financial records, and technical tools. Facts become mixed with assumptions, planning is confused with execution, receipts are lost, and repeated actions can overwrite or contradict earlier decisions.

## Product

Fardarter provides a controlled operations layer that:

- Converts fuzzy instructions into clear action packets
- Preserves facts, sources, uncertainty, and receipts
- Separates preparation from external execution
- Requires exact target, exact action, and final human approval
- Prevents duplicate actions
- Supports rollback and failed-action recovery
- Maintains continuity across work, finance, housing, legal, and technical lanes

## Initial users

Solo founders, independent professionals, and small teams that need stronger continuity, traceability, and human control over AI-assisted workflows.

## OpenAI technical fit

The prototype is intended to use OpenAI models and tools for natural-language task interpretation, structured outputs, retrieval, tool routing, multi-step workflow coordination, evaluation, and approval-controlled execution.
